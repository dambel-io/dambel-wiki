# About Us

Dambel is a comprehensive platform that connects gyms, trainers, and athletes. Everything from gym management and trainer programming to athlete performance tracking and analysis — all in one simple app.

The company, Parsa Smart Sports Innovators, started its operations in April 2024, with the mission to modernize the fitness experience and help users achieve better performance through smart technology.
