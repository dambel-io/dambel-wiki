# Profile

Your profile is what other people see when they look you up — trainers, gym owners, whoever.

![Profile screen](images/profile-screen.png)

---

## Editing your profile

Tap the **pencil icon** or **Edit Profile** button on your profile page.

You can update:
- **Display name** and username
- **Phone number** and email
- **Date of birth** (your age is calculated automatically)
- **Height** in centimeters
- **Gender** — select male, female, or other
- **Profile photo** — take a new one or pick from your gallery

Multiple profile pictures are supported. You can upload a gallery of photos that others can scroll through.

---

## Roles & badges

Your profile shows your active roles:
- **Coach** — you offer training services
- **Trainer** — you're affiliated with a gym or organization

Roles are given by Dambel — you'll get a notification if yours changes.

If you have a **Premium** membership, a premium badge appears on your profile.

---

## Trainer license

If you offer training services, upload your trainer licence here so we can verify you. If you don't train anyone, you can skip this section entirely — nothing else on your profile depends on it.

Tap **Upload license** and take a photo of the document or pick one from your gallery. Your licence then moves through one of four states:

| State | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Not uploaded** | No document yet. You can't publish training services. |
| **Awaiting review** | We've got it and our team is checking it. |
| **Approved** | You're verified and can publish training services. |
| **Rejected** | Something was wrong with the document. The reason is shown right below the status. |

If your licence is rejected, read the reason, tap **Replace license**, and upload a clearer or more current document.

You can remove the document at any time with **Remove**. Doing so also clears its approved status, so you'll need to upload a new one to be verified again. Replacing a document does the same — it goes back to awaiting review.

Your licence is private. Only you and our review team can see it; it never appears on your public profile.

You'll get a notification as soon as your licence is approved or rejected.

## Education

Add your academic background so potential clients know what you're about.

Tap **Add Education** to enter:
- School or institution name
- Field of study
- Start and end dates
- A short description

---

## Championships

Show off your competitive achievements.

Tap **Add Championship** to enter:
- Competition name
- Date
- A short description of the result

---

## Referral link

Invite friends to Dambel using your personal referral link.

On your own profile page, tap **Share Referral Link** to copy the link to your clipboard. Send it to anyone - when they open it, the registration page loads with your referral code already applied.

---

## What others see

When someone views your profile — a trainer browsing athletes, or an athlete checking a trainer — they see your name, photo, roles, bio, education, and championships. Your contact details (phone/email) are kept private.
