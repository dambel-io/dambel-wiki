# Privacy Policy

At Dambel, your privacy matters to us. Here's a clear summary of how we handle your data.

---

**What we collect**

When you use Dambel, we collect the information you provide - such as your name, email, phone number, and profile details - along with the fitness and nutrition data you log in the app. We also collect basic usage analytics to help us understand how the app is being used and improve it over time.

**How we use it**

We use your data to run the app, personalize your experience, send you relevant notifications, and improve our services. We do not use your health or fitness data for advertising.

**Sharing**

We do not sell your data. We share it only with the service providers needed to operate the app (such as cloud hosting and AI model APIs), and only when legally required.

**Security**

We use encrypted connections and industry-standard measures to keep your data safe.

**Your rights**

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at support@dambel.io. We'll get back to you within 30 days.

**Updates**

We may update this policy occasionally. If we do, we'll let you know through the app.

---

Questions? Reach us at **support@dambel.io**
